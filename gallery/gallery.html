Animal Crossing Pair (Claire and Bob)

Previous Next

Meet Bob and Town Mayor, Claire!
In the little town of Monaco no Villager goes unattended under the ever-watchful eye of Claire! Her player has spent the last *4 years playing Animal Crossing: New Leaf every day to ensure that Bob the Cat NEVER leaves. (If you talk to them and ‘ping’ them daily, they’ll eventually tell you they want to move, and you can tell them not to go. They will stay until they want to leave again) It’s not being obsessive if they’re your boyfriend, right?

Claire is sporting this lovely outfit here 

Claire and Bob’s patterns were created by shamelessly mutilating a 'Bunnie’ plush and making the pattern. They both 'stand’ at 8", but Bob’s ears reach 9". Both Bob and Claire are made of minky, Clarie’s goggles are felt and her ears and band-aids are fleece. Her dress is god-knows what. Their faces are hand-painted iron-on fabric.

Side note, now that you can use those Amiibo cards to bring in Villagers, she will never lose him! It’s-It’s not possessive, I swear.

Credit for Claire goes to my loving Sister.  Plush © Me 2015(Dec) Bob, Animal Crossing: New Leaf © Nintendo

Sans (Sitting)

Previous Next

“So you’re a human? That’s hilarious. I’m Sans, Sans the skeleton.”


Made for my friend as a Christmas gift, Sans is 18" inches tall. His pattern was made from scratch and inspired by the magnificent sewbro.tumblr.com. His body is Alova, his coat, pants and ketchup are minky, his slippers are fluffy and felt. His zipper is perfectly functional and he is wearing a white turtle-neck underneath. His face and Tomato sticker are hand-painted iron-on fabric.

Enjoy him, friend, make sure he drinks his ketchup responsibly!
